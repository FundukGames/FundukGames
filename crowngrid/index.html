What is CrownGrid?

CrownGrid is a browser-based logic puzzle in the same family as classic crown / "queens" placement games. The board is split into colored regions. Your goal is to place exactly one crown in each region, while also keeping exactly one crown per row and per column — and making sure no two crowns are adjacent, not even diagonally. Every board has a single, logically deducible solution.

Unlike the puzzles built into big platforms, CrownGrid is truly unlimited: generate a brand-new board with one click, choose your difficulty from 6×6 up to 8×8, and play as many times as you like without signing in.

How to play (60-second version)

  1. Tap a cell once to place an ✕ — a note that a crown can't go there.
  2. Tap again to place a ♛ crown. Tap a third time to clear the cell.
  3. You need one crown per region, row and column.
  4. No two crowns may touch — not horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  5. Conflicting crowns are outlined in red so you can fix mistakes fast.

Frequently asked questions

Is CrownGrid free?
Yes — completely free, and supported by ads.
Do I need an account or login?
No — no registration and no sign-up. Just open the page and play; your streak and best times are saved in your browser.
Is there a daily puzzle?
Yes — the Daily Challenge gives everyone the same board each day and tracks your streak. Want more? Hit "New puzzle" for unlimited random boards.
How is this different from the LinkedIn Queens game?
CrownGrid is an independent game that uses the same well-known logic mechanic (one crown per region/row/column, none touching). It is not affiliated with LinkedIn and lets you play unlimited boards without an account.
