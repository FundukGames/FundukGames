What is FreeCell?

FreeCell is a Solitaire variant where all 52 cards are dealt face up into eight columns. Four free cells each hold a single card as temporary storage. Because nothing is hidden, almost every deal is solvable with the right sequence of moves — it's a thinking player's Solitaire.

Our version is unlimited and free: deal as many games as you like, drag and drop or tap to auto-move, undo freely, and play without an account.

How to play (60-second version)

  1. Build the foundations up by suit, Ace → King.
  2. Build the columns down in alternating colours.
  3. Each free cell holds one card — your scratch space.
  4. You can move a run of cards if you have enough free cells and empty columns to do it.
  5. Any card may go onto an empty column.

Frequently asked questions

Is FreeCell free?
Yes — completely free, supported by ads, no login or sign-up.
Do I need an account?
No — play without registration; your stats and daily streak are saved in your browser.
Are all FreeCell games winnable?
Almost all of them. Because every card is visible, the vast majority of deals can be solved with careful play.
How do I move several cards at once?
You can move a run as long as you have enough free cells and empty columns. The game only lets you drop a run that fits the limit.
Is there a daily puzzle?
Yes — the Daily Challenge deals everyone the same game each day and tracks your streak.
