About Funduk Games

Funduk Games is a tiny independent studio with one stubborn belief: a good puzzle shouldn't make you sign up, download anything, or wait until tomorrow. So everything here is free, runs straight in your browser, and plays as long as you like. We started with CrownGrid, a crown-placement logic puzzle, and kept adding games we actually wanted to play ourselves — Sudoku, the Tango-style SunMoon, the one-line Trail, three classic Solitaires, and Nutfall, a 3D nut-dropping arcade.

Every logic puzzle is generated to have a single solution you can reach by pure deduction — no guessing, no luck. We keep the lights on with ads, and we write honestly about the ups and downs of building this on Patreon.
