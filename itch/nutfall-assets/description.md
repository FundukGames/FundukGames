## Description (rich text body)
**Nutfall** is a 3D Plinko-meets-idle game. A squirrel tosses nuts down a tall tree trunk — they tumble under gravity, bounce off the obstacles **you** place, and pile up your harvest in the hollows below.

There are no fixed pegs. You design the board:

- **+N / ×N modifiers** pump up a nut's value as it falls
- **Bumpers** speed nuts up and route the flow
- **Tornado funnels** pull nuts into your best lane
- **Merge** two identical modifiers into a stronger tier
- **Upgrade each hollow's multiplier** and steer the rich nuts into it

Every level is a fresh board with bigger caps and a bigger goal — a light roguelike loop where your unlocks carry over but the machine is rebuilt from scratch. How efficient can you make it?
